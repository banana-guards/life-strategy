## Description

In a world where daily decisions are often driven by urgency and intuition, this application proposes a different approach: **thinking strategically about your personal life**.

Based on the article _“Use Strategic Thinking to Create the Life You Want”_ by Rainer Strack, Susanne Dyrchs, and Allison Bailey, the app guides users through key questions that help clarify what truly matters.

---
## Problem It Solves

Many people:

- Lack clarity about their long-term goals
- Make decisions based on emotions or immediate pressure
- Don’t have tools to plan their life strategically
